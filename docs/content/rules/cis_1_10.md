# cis_1_10 — Ensure no more than 3 subscription Owners exist

!!! note "Summary"
    **Severity:** :material-alert-circle-outline: Medium · **Pillar:** Identity · **Chain role:** ENABLER

## Description

Too many Owner role assignments inflate the blast radius of a credential compromise. Keep no more than 3 Owner assignments per subscription.
